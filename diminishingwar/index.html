Diminishing War

Diminishing War explores a world locked in endless conflict where magic, soldiers, and even reality itself are being slowly worn away.

The flow of magic is diminishing throught the world of Orra. The City of Magic glows dimmer, Forests blooms wither, The Dwarvern Runes grows weaker, The Swamps waters stagnate, The sun scorches the sands, Tension between nations rises. Enemies sound the drums of war. Support from Allies is quite. It is up to you to protect your people, and your homeland.
