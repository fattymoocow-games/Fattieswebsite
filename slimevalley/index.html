Slime Valley

Slime Valley Is a breeding, farming, and exploring game. The creatures you breed are slimes, and the game focuses on 8 primary elements: fire, water, earth, air, nature, death, light, and dark. Using these elemets, and can create a variety of different slimes!

You will use the slimes to help you farm special food for them that will augment their latent powers. This will leads to better and stronger slimes to help you fight, explore, and increase Their proficiencies are around the home and farm!
