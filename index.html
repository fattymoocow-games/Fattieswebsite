About Me

I am an Indie game developer. I am programmer first and not an artist. So most thing will not look good right away. I am currently working on 2 projects, Slime Vally, and Dimiishing War. Slime valley currently has the most focus. Slime Valley Is a breeding, farming, and exploring game. The creatures you breed are slimes, and the game focuses on 8 primary elements: fire, water, earth, air, nature, death, light, and dark. Using these elemets, and can create a variety of different slimes!

The second game is more passivly worked on at the moment and is Diminishing War. This Game is a strategy war game were you choose you faction, and conquer the world. It is an introduction to my univers I am building and plan to have other games in (Slime Valley is technically in this world too!). Diminishing war has different faction that each have their own units and mechanics. Below are socials, but currently don't use them too much.
